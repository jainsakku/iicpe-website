IICPE 2018 is being organized under the aegis of IEEE PES-IAS Delhi Chapter as well as IEEE PELS-IES Delhi Chapter. It aims to provide a premier platform for electrical engineers and scientists in universities, research centers and industry to present their work and to share experiences and ideas in the areas of power and energy engineering.

The conference is co-sponsored by IEEE and MNIT. IICPE 2018 invites authors to submit original research papers and original work-in-progress reports on power and energy engineering.

Topic Areas:

The topics of interest for IICPE-2018 include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • Power Electronics and Energy Conversion
  • Electrical Machines and Drives
  • Renewable Energy Systems
  • Energy Storage and Battery Management
  • Transportation Electrification and Vehicle Systems
  • Smart Grid Technologies
  • Control System, Robotics, Mechatronics and Automation
  • Electronic Devices and Circuits

About MNIT

Subsequently; on June 26, 2002 the college has been given the status of National Institute of Technology and on 15 August 2007, Proclaimed Institute of
